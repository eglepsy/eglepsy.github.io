Practical information

Duration and rates

Type Duration Rate
Individual sessions 1h 30€
Couple’s session 2h 60€
Expat women support group 2h 15€

Usually individual sessions are scheduled every 1 week, couple sessions every 2-4 weeks, expat women support group – every 2 weeks.

Always first 1-2 sessions are the intake ones, made for discussing the needs, setting the goals and deciding on the fitting treatment. The first sessions are also needed to create the bond and check how do we feel with each other.

The length of the counseling varies by each case, but approximately individual counseling takes 8 sessions per goal (then the progress and other goals are checked); couple’s counseling takes min 2-3 sessions; the expat women support group is long-term, requiring min 6 months commitment (essential for creating the bond and trusting environment to work on personal issues).

Registration and cancellation

To register for any of the sessions, contact me via the information provided in the contact page.

The cancellation/ re-scheduling is possible to later than 48 hours before the agreed time, otherwise the full rate will be charged.

Messaging and phone calls

Contacting via phone, e-mail is limited to the exchange of information before the intake session and discussion about re-scheduling. No e-mail, message or phone counseling is provided.
